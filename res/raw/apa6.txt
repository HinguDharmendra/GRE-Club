<p style="text-align: justify;">
	Planning to pursue higher studies in USA often gets complicated as one doesn&rsquo;t know on what basis he/she should select a university. Selection of a university depends on the following factors:</p>
<ol>
	<li style="text-align: justify;">
		Course/Stream</li><br/>
	<li style="text-align: justify;">
		On-Campus Placements</li><br/>
	<li style="text-align: justify;">
		Tuition Fee and Living Expenses</li><br/>
	<li style="text-align: justify;">
		Location (<em>advantage in getting Off Campus Job</em>)</li><br/>
	<li style="text-align: justify;">
		Acceptance rate of International students</li><br/>
	<li style="text-align: justify;">
		Weather</li><br/>
	<li style="text-align: justify;">
		Friends/Classmates</li><br/>
	<li style="text-align: justify;">
		Relatives</li><br/>
</ol>
<p style="text-align: justify;">
	Of these the most important factor is Course/Stream. Your future is decided by the course you opt for. Ask your seniors who have been there in the previous Fall and tell them about your course.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">
	<em><strong>Result: </strong></em>You will get an ideal list of universities as they themselves must have surveyed a lot.</p>
